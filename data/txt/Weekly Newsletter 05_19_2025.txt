Dear CACC Chinese School Families,
All classes for the 2024–2025 academic year at CACC Chinese School have successfully concluded. On behalf of all teachers and office staff, I would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to all students and parents: thank you for choosing to study at CACC Chinese School and for your continued support!
Starting May 16, registration for the 2025–2026 academic year is now open to all students and the public. You may log in to your account to register for your preferred courses. If you have any questions during the summer, feel free to contact us at cs@caccusa.org.
Below are some important registration dates and basic policies:
* Tuition:
   * Discounted Price: Full payment must be made before July 1, 2025.
   * Regular Price: Begins on July 1, 2025.
* Registration Fee:   $30 per academic year (non-refundable if a class is dropped).
* Volunteer Deposit: $50 per family (refundable). Please see our Volunteer FAQ for details.

* For more registration dates and rules, please visit: https://www.caccusa.org/school.php
