Subject: 伯克利迷你数学竞赛报名 & 2月16日休课一周/ Berkeley Mini Math Tournament & No Class on Feb. 16th.


尊敬的中文学校家长们：


2025 年伯克利迷你数学竞赛（ BmMT） 现场比赛将于 4 月 12 日星期日在加州大学伯克利分校举行。比赛包括 4 个主要回合: 图形、个人、团队和接力赛。BmMT 面向 8 年级或以下的中学生开放。小学高年级学生也可以参加比赛。有关比赛的更多信息，请访问：BmMT 官网


考试日期：2025 年 4 月 12 日。上午 7:30 至晚上 7:30。在此期间还将有额外的学生和家长活动。具体细节尚未公布
注册费：每名学生 15 美元。现金在 CACC 办公室（Q215 室）收取。
注册截止日期：2025 年 2 月 23 日。
团队规模：每队最多 5 名成员。学生可以自己组队，也可以由 CACC 工作人员分组。
赛前辅导：CACC 将提供四节免费在线辅导课。
交通和竞赛当日现场组织：由学生家长承担。
练习题：https://berkeley.mt/archives/
注册链接：注册2025 BmMT 


2月16日学校休课一周，23日恢复上课。


Dear CACC families，


The 2025 BmMT in-person competition will be held at the UC Berkeley campus on Sunday, April 12th. The tournament consists of 4 main rounds — Puzzle, Individual, Team, and Relay. The BmMT is open to middle school students in grades 8 or below.  Advanced elementary school students are also welcome to compete. For more information about the competition, please visit:  https://berkeley.mt/events/bmmt-2025/


Test Date: April 12th, 2025. 7:30am to 7:30pm. There will also be additional student and parent activities during this time period. Specific details have not been announced yet
Registration Fee: $15 per student. Cash collected in the CACC office (room Q215). 
Registration Deadline: Feb 23, 2025. 
Team Size: Up to 5 members per team. Students can make their own team or be grouped by CACC staff. 
Coaching: CACC will provide four free online tutoring classes.
Transportation and Onsite Organization on the Tournament Day: Up to the parents of the students. 
Practice Questions: https://berkeley.mt/archives/
Registration link: Registration 


No School on Feb.16 and Class Will Resume on Feb.23.
