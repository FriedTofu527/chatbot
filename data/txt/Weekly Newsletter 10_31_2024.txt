Teacher Weekly Newsletter 10/31/2024


Subject： 夏时制结束/ 晚会节目征集


各位CACC中文学校的老师们：
夏时制：
将在本周末11月3号结束。请在周六晚上调整您的时钟，您有多一个小时的休息时间。
晚会节目征集：


1. 2025年蛇年春节联欢会将于2025年2月2日下午在Amador Valley High School举行。在这个迎春活动中，中文学校将准备丰富多彩的内容目，包括精彩表演、传统游戏、美味小吃以及拍照留念等。 
2. 为弘扬中华文化、加强社区联系并服务更多家庭，CACC将举办一场面向社区的文化演出，这场演出将于2025年3月22日于Danville Village Theater举行，欢迎校内校外观众一同前来观赏。


如果您或您的学生有意参与演出，请准备好节目申请。稍后我们将通过邮件和微信发布节目申请表格，敬请关注。 期待与大家共度这两场难忘的活动！


Dear CACC Chinese School teachers:


Daylight Savings Time:
Daylight Savings Time ends this Sunday on November 3rd. Please remember to set your clocks back one hour.


Spring Festival Gala Show:


1. The 2025 Spring Festival Gala will be held at Amador Valley High School on the afternoon of February 2, 2025. The Chinese school will prepare a variety of content, including wonderful performances, traditional games, delicious foods, and fun photography events.


2. In order to promote Chinese culture, strengthen community ties, and serve more families, CACC will hold an additional cultural performance for the community. This event will be held at Danville Village Theater on March 22, 2025. Audiences from both inside and outside the school are welcome to come and watch.


If you or your students are interested in participating in the performance, please prepare a program application. We will release the program application form via email and WeChat later, so stay tuned. We look forward to spending these two unforgettable events with you!
