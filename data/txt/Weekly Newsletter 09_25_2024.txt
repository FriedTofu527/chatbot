The AMC 8 is a 25-question, 40-minute, multiple-choice examination in middle school mathematics designed to promote the development and enhancement of problem-solving skills. To learn test problems and solutions of previous years please visit  AMC 8 Problems and Solutions


Competition Method: Thomas Hart Middle School In-person test 


Competition Date: Jan. 23 2025 .


Registration Deadline:  Oct. 18th, 2024 


Registration Link: AMC 8 2025  


Registration Fee: $9 per student. Cash collected at the office (Q215) on Oct. 13th, 20th, 27th. The registration fee is not refundable.


*** Most middle schools in the bay area will host the AMC 8 competition for their students. Please ask your math teacher or math club if they host the competition before registering with CACC Chinese School. We strongly recommend that students take the test at their own school. Thomas Hart Middle School only provides testing opportunities to those whose school is not hosting the test. Please note that one student can only register on one test site and take one test.
