ACSL Competition and New Course Launch Info Session:
The American Computer Science League (ACSL) organizes an annual computer science competition for K–12 students. This year, our students formed a team to participate in the competition.  To encourage more students to participate, we will offer a new course for the 2025–2026 school year to help students prepare for the ACSL. We are pleased to invite Dr. Xiaofei Ma, our team coach, to introduce the ACSL competition, share his experiences, and Dr. Ping Zhang to discuss our new class offering. 
 Time: April 27, 10:00 AM – 11:00 AM
Location: Classroom Q205
Speakers: Dr. Ping Zhang, Dr. Xiaofei Ma
2025–2026 School Year Dates:
* First Day of School: August 17, 2025

* Last Day of School: May 17, 2026

* Registration Dates:
   * Priority Registration Period starting at 05/03/25: Current teachers, room parents, and office staff, current and retired BOD members.
   * Regular Registration Period starting at 05/09/25: all currently enrolled students/families

   * Public Registration Period starting at 05/16/25 : all returning and new students/families

   * Early registration discount will be applied for students who have enrolled and paid in full by 11:59 pm on 06/30/25.
