Transfer of High School Chinese Credit:
The fall semester of High School Chinese Credit Classes ended on January 5, 2025. Our school has sent the original transcripts and email notifications to the school counselors for the students who requested a credit transfer. If you have any questions about when your score will appear on your report card, please contact your high school counselor.
