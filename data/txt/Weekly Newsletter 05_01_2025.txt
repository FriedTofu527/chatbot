Subject：5月11日校园开放日/ May 11 Open House Day — Celebrate Our Students' Growth!


各位中文学校的家长们，


5月11日将是本学年的最后一天课程（高中学分班除外)，我们将在这一天举行校园开放日活动，诚邀您来学校和自己的孩子上最后一次课，见证他们这一年的进步和成长。


您可以：
* 进入自己孩子的教室旁听最后一节课，观看班级颁奖、表演、游戏活动。
* 参观校园里摆放的学生作品、作业展览。
* 观摩学校里的开放课程。（详情见后面列表）
* 参加理事会组织的家长早餐会，畅所欲言，为中文学校发展献计献策。
* 参加10:00 am-12:00 pm CACC & X-Camp 计算机新课发布会。
* 了解下学年的课程，注册答疑。
* 咨询赞助商提供的暑假活动


* 我校摄影班的学员会为部分教室的活动提供摄影服务，记录下美好时刻，照片会上传到特定云盘仅供本班家长下载。

Dear CACC Chinese School Parents,
May 11 will be the last day of the 2025-2026 school year (except for the high school credit classes). We will hold an Open House Day event on that day, and we sincerely invite you to come to the school, attend your child’s final class, and witness their progress and growth over the past year.
You are welcome to:
* Sit in on your child’s final class and watch class awards, performances, and games.

* Visit the students' work and assignments exhibition on the campus.

* Observe classroom showcases (details listed below).

* Attend the Parent Breakfast organized by the School Board, sharing your thoughts and suggestions.

* Join the CACC & X-Camp New Computer Course Launch Event from 10:00 am to 12:00 pm.

* Consult with sponsors about summer activities.

* Learn more about next year's courses and ask questions about registration.

*Students from our photography class will provide photography services for activities in some classrooms, capturing exciting moments.  Photos will be uploaded to a designated cloud drive, exclusively available for parents of each class to download.
